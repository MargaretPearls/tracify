HOW IT WORKS

Gain more insight into how
our service works

Everything you need to streamline your logistics operations
and improve on-time delivery:

1

Order capture

Capture orders and shipment information directly from host systems such as transport Management Systems (TMS), Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Warehouse Management system (WMS) into the TraciFy Platform. No manual intervention to avoid errors and associated inefficiencies.

2

Shipment tracking

Be in control. Get Shipments location and status information of in-transit shipments.

3

Predictive ETA

Your Customers know when to expect their shipments. Our advanced algorithm predicts ETAs using live and historical in-transit information with external factors like traffic and weather.

4

Proof of Delivery

Create and share instant Proof of Delivery (POD) with customers, shippers and carriers thereby making it easy to manage and reconcile customer refusals seamlessly and in real-time.

5

Scan to load

Improve traceability of SKUs from warehouse up to the customer locations. Increase truck loading efficiency by eliminating staging and manual pallets/SKU validation. Our automated barcode scanning technology is highly optimized for this task.

6

Messaging/alerts

Get real-time notifications for delays and status changes for in-transit shipments to enhance prompt decision making

7

Dashboards

Get a complete picture of your operations and performance, and insights into potential issues. All from a single, easy-to-manage dashboard presenting different views of data and reporting.

8

Carrier network/integration

Connect to multiple Carriers directly for tracking information independent of devices or mobile applications. Leverage our existing carrier network to reduce time to connect Carriers.

9

Customizable

Every business is unique. We provide you a highly customizable solutions that you can tailor to your needs with minimal efforts.

10

Fuel allocation/monitoring

automatically allocate fuel based on distance and truck type. Remotely monitor utilization of allocated fuel and get instant notifications for unexpected fuel drop or theft.
